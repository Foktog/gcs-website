Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Golden Camp Solutions

2018

Foundation

Golden Camp Solutions established with first contract at Kibali Gold Mine, DRC, partnering with Barrick Gold. This marked the beginning of our mission to deliver world-class camp services to Africa's mining sector.

2019

DRC Growth

Expanded operations at KGM to include housekeeping, laundry, shop, bar and restaurant services. This expansion demonstrated our capability to deliver integrated, full-service camp management solutions.

2021

Zambia Launch

Secured major contract at Lumwana Mining Company — GCS's largest site to date, serving 3,980+ meals per day. This milestone represented significant growth and strategic presence in the Copperbelt Province.

2024

Uganda Expansion

Extended operations into Uganda, marking our presence across three African nations. This expansion positions GCS as a truly continental player in remote-site services delivery.
