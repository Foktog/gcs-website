Our Story

Built from the Ground Up in Africa's Toughest Terrain

Golden Camp Solutions was founded in 2018 by Roy Crouch to take over the catering and camp management contract for the Kibali Gold Mine in North Eastern DRC — one of Africa's largest gold mining operations. From day one, the commitment was simple: deliver exceptional service in remote conditions, no excuses.

In September 2021, GCS extended its operations to Zambia, providing catering and camp management services for the Lumwana Mining Company, a Barrick Gold operation in the Copperbelt Province. Operations in Uganda followed in 2022, completing a three-country footprint across sub-Saharan Africa.

Today GCS employs 571 dedicated staff and serves over 6,197 meals daily across its operations — built on a foundation of quality, safety and a genuine commitment to the communities in which we work.
