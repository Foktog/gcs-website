Real Stories. Real Impact.

Community in Action

Golden Camp Solutions actively participates in the communities where we operate. We believe that healthy, empowered communities build a more productive and sustainable workforce.

🌸

International Women's Day — Zambia

GCS contributed catering for the International Women's Day celebration hosted by the local government in Zambia's Copperbelt district. The event was well attended and provided an opportunity to celebrate and recognise the tremendous contribution of women in our communities and our workforce. At GCS we appreciate all that our female employees do for the company and their continued efforts to build the GCS brand.

🌾

Local Farmer Partnerships

Across our shop and restaurant operations, GCS actively sources from local community farmers — giving small-scale producers access to a reliable, large-scale buyer. This partnership model supports food security, stimulates local economies and ensures our clients receive fresh, locally-grown produce.

Our Commitments

How We're Building a Sustainable Future

🥗

Local Food Sourcing

We partner with local farmers and suppliers wherever possible, reducing food miles and supporting community livelihoods. Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients fuel our operations.

♻️

Waste Reduction

Across all sites we implement food waste minimisation and responsible disposal protocols aligned with best environmental practice. Every meal counts.

👩‍👧

Women in the Workforce

GCS actively promotes gender inclusion, ensuring women play key roles across our operations from kitchen to management. Diversity strengthens our teams.

💧

Water & Energy

Our laundry and kitchen operations use water-efficient, energy-conscious processes to minimise environmental footprint. Conservation is a daily practice.

🏘️

Community Employment

We prioritise hiring from local communities, channelling wages and economic benefits directly into the areas around our sites. Communities first.

📋

QHSE Standards

Strict Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental standards are applied uniformly across all GCS operations and sites. Excellence is non-negotiable.
