World-Class Services at One of Africa's Largest Gold Mines

Kibali Gold Mine (KGM) is located in the northeast of the Democratic Republic of Congo — the third biggest investment in the DRC and a major catalyst in transforming the Haut-Uélé province economically. GCS has been the trusted camp services partner at KGM since 2018.

With close to 3,000 employees on site, GCS provides comprehensive catering for all day and night shift staff, alongside housekeeping, laundry, a shop, a licensed bar and restaurant. Our team of 256 dedicated staff ensures seamless delivery of over 54,960 meals per month.

GCS's Largest Operation — Powering Zambia's Copper Heartland

Lumwana Mining Company (LMC) is a copper mine located approximately 100km west of Solwezi in Zambia's Copperbelt Province, employing around 5,000 people. GCS has been delivering catering and housekeeping services since 2021, growing this into our largest active operation.

Our Lumwana team of 372 staff serves an average of 3,980 meals per day and services 1,532 beds daily. The GCS team also manages 23,000 square metres of facilities cleaned per day — one of the most intensive cleaning programmes on the continent.

Additional Services: In addition to core services, GCS operates a fully stocked shop with 600+ items and a cashless payment system, a licensed bar and restaurant, and full guest management services including computerised and manual occupancy systems.

Uganda Operations — Active and Growing

GCS operates in Uganda with 8 dedicated staff providing catering, housekeeping and restaurant services. Our Uganda team currently serves 90 meals per day, representing an important part of our growth strategy in East Africa.

Our Uganda presence forms part of our long-term strategy to expand GCS's footprint across East and Central Africa, bringing the same level of operational excellence and community partnership that has defined our success in the DRC and Zambia.
