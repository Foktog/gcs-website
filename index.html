Who We Are

Built for the Demands of Africa's Mining Sector

Since 2018, Golden Camp Solutions has grown from a single site into a multi-country operation, delivering exceptional camp services to some of Africa's most significant mining projects including Barrick Gold's Kibali Gold Mine in the DRC and the Lumwana Mining Company in Zambia.

We understand that a well-fed, well-rested and well-serviced workforce is a productive one. Our agile, dedicated team operates with an unwavering commitment to quality, safety and the communities in which we work.
