Who We Are

Built for the Demands of Africa's Mining Sector

Since 2018, Golden Camp Solutions has grown from a single site into a multi-country operation, delivering integrated camp services to some of Africa's most significant mining projects in the DRC, Zambia and Uganda.

A well-fed, well-rested workforce is a productive one. Our team operates with an unwavering commitment to quality, safety and the communities in which we work.

Proven Track Record

6+ years of continuous service delivery across DRC, Zambia and Uganda.

Reliable Partner

Community First

Full Compliance

Strict QHSE, hygiene and safety protocols enforced across all sites.
