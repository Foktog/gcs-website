Our Services

From the first meal of the day to a clean, comfortable room at night — end-to-end camp services tailored to Africa's mining sector.

Catering Services

2 million+ meals per year across day and night shifts. Culturally diverse menus, onsite bakery, packed lunches and mass catering — all to the highest nutritional standards.

Housekeeping & Laundry

Daily room servicing, computer-controlled laundry, weekly linen replacement and ISO-compliant deep cleaning — immaculate standards across every accommodation block.

Guest Management

Professional check-in and check-out, computerised occupancy tracking, VIP and government guest handling, 24/7 front-of-house coverage and full emergency protocol management.

Shop & Restaurant

Cashless card shops stocked with 3,000+ items, fully licensed bar and restaurant, community farmer sourcing, satellite plant-site shops and event banqueting.

Camp Management

End-to-end facility management: dedicated camp manager, occupancy reporting, staff scheduling, maintenance coordination and QHSE compliance — from first arrival to demobilisation.

Community & Sustainability

Local hiring, community farmer partnerships, waste reduction and environmentally responsible practices — creating lasting value in every region we operate.
