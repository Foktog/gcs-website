Catering Services

Our full catering solution serves over 2 million meals a year, recognising the vital role food plays in sustaining a productive workforce. Our dedicated culinary team ensures clients receive diverse, nutritious and delicious meals tailored to their needs.

We understand that food fuels not just the body but also the mind and soul. Ethnic and religious dietary preferences are accommodated in our daily menu offerings, with specialist chefs often recruited from your workforce's own countries of origin. We also operate onsite bakeries to ensure fresh products are available every day.

Service Features

  • Large menu choices with customised local specialities
  • Consistent high-quality food and nutritional standards
  • High-control ticket/scanning system for all clients served
  • Delivery to plant sites in containership
  • Day and night shift meal provision
  • Braais, barbecues and special event catering
  • Onsite bakery producing fresh bread, pastries and desserts
  • Local and community sourcing programmes

Housekeeping & Laundry

We understand the importance of maintaining pristine cleanliness across all areas of your remote-site facility. Our expert housekeeping services are tailored to meet the highest standards, ensuring a hygienic and inviting environment for residents and employees alike.

Our state-of-the-art laundry facilities employ computer-controlled chemical and automated processes. Laundry is collected from rooms daily, marked with tags and documented for tracking. All bedding is washed on a weekly basis with replacements fitted on the day of the wash cycle.

Service Features

  • High-standard housekeeping every 3 days with consumable replenishment
  • Daily laundry collection, tagging and tracking
  • Automated computer-controlled laundry processing
  • Weekly bedding replacement programme
  • COVID-19 disinfection and sanitation services
  • Workstation, accommodation, dining area and kitchen deep-clean
  • Full turnkey alterations service
  • ISO-compliant hygiene protocols
Daily Laundry Room Servicing Deep Cleaning Linen Management Disinfection ISO Compliance

Guest Management

Our guest management system ensures every employee and visitor receives a seamless, professional arrival and departure experience. From government guests to new recruits — every person is welcomed, informed and accommodated to the highest standard.

Service Features

  • Professional check-in and check-out at reception
  • Room/tent key issuance and escorted orientation
  • Rules, emergency contacts, site map and facility briefing for all new guests
  • Computerised and manual occupancy tracking at all times
  • Government and VIP guest management
  • Room allocation and bed management
  • Emergency protocol coordination
  • 24/7 front-of-house availability

Shop & Restaurant

GCS operates vibrant onsite shops and fully licensed restaurants and bars that are the heart of the camp community. Our shops carry over 600 items with a cashless card system for convenient transactions. We believe in connecting our clients with the local economy — partnering with nearby farmers and community suppliers to stock fresh, locally-sourced products.

Service Features

  • Cashless card payment system for all shop transactions
  • 600+ product lines covering everyday essentials
  • Satellite shop at plant site mine area for night-shift convenience
  • Community farmer partnerships and local sourcing
  • Fully licensed bar and restaurant operations
  • A la carte and light meal menus
  • Diverse cuisine covering local and international dishes
  • Comprehensive event planning and banqueting

Camp Management

Our camp management service provides complete, end-to-end oversight of your remote site facility. From the moment the first person arrives to final demobilisation, GCS manages every operational dimension — ensuring your camp runs safely, efficiently and in full compliance with all applicable standards.

Service Features

  • Dedicated Camp Manager assigned from first arrival to demobilisation
  • Full occupancy management and reporting
  • Staff scheduling, supervision and performance management
  • Maintenance coordination and facility upkeep
  • QHSE compliance monitoring and incident reporting
  • Supply chain and procurement coordination
  • Operational vehicle for site logistics
  • Stakeholder and client liaison
